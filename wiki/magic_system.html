Magic System

Wiki > Player guide > Magic system

Magic is conducted via a series of emotes. Usually it takes between 2-4 emotes to cast a spell depending on the level of that spell and proficiency.

  1. An emote to channel the magic aura must be done. If you are casting magic outside of combat, this must be done first.
  2. The number of emotes used to cast a spell may vary depending on the user's proficiency of the spell, and difficulty level of the spell.
  3. Roll for hit and damage. Modifiers are added for rolls depending on power, items, proficiency, etc... The defending mage may attempt to block or deflect the spell, and will have to roll as well.

Magic Types

Wiki > Player guide > Magic system > Magic types

Magic is catergorized into 3 groups: Incantations, Sorcery, and Alchemic.

Each group is further split into subcatergories as shown below.

Incantations

Incantations are abstract in nature and call upon both natural and spiritual elements.

Miracles

Similar to dark souls, miracles are a type of incantation that has godly principles, able to drawn on power depending on one's faith and belief.

Blessings are buffs that may be given to any living creature (creatures considered living are those with a soul).

Restorations can heal injuries, both physical and spiritual.

Weapons of Faith are versatile auras that the user may summon, ranging from defensive, offensive, or support. Some examples of this are armour, swords, or even wings.

Pyromancy

Summoning the elemental power of fire is conducted via incantation.

Magic fire is hard to control, but it is flexible and can be wielded in many ways. The most common use of pyromancy is melding fire into projectiles such as fireballs or spears.

However, pyromancy is not limited to offensive measures, and may be used in defensive situations.

There are different types of fire that may be channeled by a user. The only lawful flame in Luthwardron is holy fire.

Eldritch Incantations

Magic that is drawn from the faith and belief in a powerful entity. Most often eldritch incantations are a mix of miracles and elemental magics.

This magic has been outlawed as taboo in Luthwardron.

Spiritual Incantations

Drawing upon the power of spirits, ghosts, or creatures.

This magic is forbidden to students.

Druid Magic relates to the nature of the world and is drawn by the power of life.

Bardmancy is drawn from stories to instill positive emotions in allies or negative emotions in enemies. It is often used by bards as a dramatic flair in shows, hence the name.

Sorcery

The use of advanced techniques to refine natural elements into tools and weapons.

Incantations are the primordial form of magic, relying on faith. Sorcery relies on a deeper understanding of the world and its workings.

That being said, Sorcery can be drawn by a multitude of sources. It is typically drawn from a concept that can be studied and formulated.

The power of sorcery may be multiplied by objects such as rings and staffs.

Sorcery is commonly drawn from starlight, moonlight, dark light, and elemental sources. Often a mix is used for spells.

Offensive Sorcery

Projectiles are the most common type of offensive magic. It may be molded into many shapes such as arrows, boulders, and the like.

Conjurations take the shape of weapons and acts in similar ways to projectiles. Conjured weapons and tools can hold the shape and properties of their summoned form until it wears down or the sorcerer is unable to hold it.

Wards are magical barriers that can be summoned to deflect or block incoming attacks.

Elemental magic such as ice, lightning, water, and others can be summoned. Elements are often used as projectiles.

Storms are a form of magic that summons large auras of magic that harms whoever steps inside.

Domestic Sorcery

Sorcery that is not used for the improvement of life rather than for combat.

Conjuration may be a form of domestic sorcery. A great understanding of the conjured object must be achieved for it to be proper.

Illusion Craft uses sources of light and elements to cast illusions of things that are not there. Unlike conjugations, Illusions only require visual understanding. They do not hold up to physical contact like conjurations.

Dark Light manipulates objects. Objects can be made to float or fly, or be tossed about. It is useful for travel and labor.

Enchantment imbues objects with magic. Some examples of this are wards to protect the object, or programmed spells that can be slung from swords.

Charm Crafting is similar to enchantment. Charms are talismans that enhance magical power. They are hard to make as each must be fine tuned to the specific person. If multiple are used without calculating their effects it can lead to bad results such as sudden combustion.

Altercation is the ability to alter oneself with magic to improve the senses. It is possible to use altercation to see and feel things previously unable to be.

Alchemy

The study of physical objects and elements and the reactions of chemicals. In this magic system, matter cannot be created from of nothing.

Transmutation is the chemical change of one object into another. The very properties of the object is altered into another form.

Transfiguration is the altercation of the physical appearance and or shape of an object. The chemical properties remain the same but the shape is altered.

Potion Brewery is the creation of mixtures and concoctions by mixing elements together. Powerful but temporary effects can be granted to oneself, as well as potent venoms or poisons.
