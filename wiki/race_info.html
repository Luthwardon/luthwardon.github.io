Playable Races

Wiki > Race Guide > Playable Races

Human

Humans are the most populace race, driven by their ambition to become adaptable in nearly any condition.

Luethenians are those born in the heart of Luthwardron. They are born with strong proficiency in holy magics.

Outlanders are those hailing from foreign lands, not yet subdued by Luthwardron. They are born with strong proficiency in spiritual incantations.

Dwarf

A dying race in Luthwardron, they live in small sanctuary throughout the lands, having lost their homelands long ago.

They are born with stronger proficiency in elemental sorceries. They also are naturally gifted artisans.

Half-Elf

The original elves are long since gone from the lands of Luthwardron, but remnants of their existence persist in the form of half-elves. Often hailing from noble blood, half-elves are slightly more proficient at all magic and slightly stronger than their human counterparts.

Tiefling

They are born of human blood that has been tainted by demonic influence. They are banished deep beneath the earth for their unholy ties, in the under realm. Tieflings are naturally gifted in pyromancy, and they also are gifted in specific elemental magic, depending on their blood.

Tieflings are persecuted on the surface, and if exposed by any authority their sentence is death.

Halfling

Physically weak, but swift and nimble. Halflings are protected in Luthwardron, living in small shire communes throughout the land. They are naturally gifted in brewery and have some improved strength in spiritual magic.
