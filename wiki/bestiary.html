Creature types

Beasts are basic animals such as fish, mammals, and birds.

Humanoids are bipedal creatures that (usually) have comparably higher intelligence, such as ogres and goblins.

Giants can be loosely-decribed as "boss monters", such as dragons and dragon-class creatures.

Constructs are unnatural creatures which are conjured, summoned, or built, such as golems and familiars.

Plant-like creatures are lower-intelligence lifeforms, such as slimes, or creatures with ties to plants.

Undead creatures were once alive but have been reanimated with a magical force, such as ghosts, skeletons, and spirits.

Horrors are unnatural creatures with strange origins. Creatures that do not fit into other categories are here.
