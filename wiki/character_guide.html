Stats

Wiki > Player guide > Character building > Stats

Depending on what you spec your stats into, your character will be more proficient at different skills.

Below is a basic rundown of each stat.

Strength is a measure of your character's physical capability. It increases proficiency in heavy weapons and armour.

Dexterity is a measure of your character's physical precision and speed. It increases proficiency in light weapons and armour.

Vigor is a measure of your character's resistance to attacks and the environment. It increases your character's health.

Faith increases proficiency in incantations.

Intelligence is a measure of your character's knowledge. It increases proficiency in sorcery.

Wisdom is a measure of your character's intuition. It increases proficiency in sorcery.

During open host, you do not have to calculate character stats, and instead will be given a basic set of stats. The character creation is optional for people who want to keep their progress.

When you make a character, you will get some stats to devote points into. It will be kept simple and the rp won't heavily depend on stats.

Similar to D&D, you will roll an amount equal to the amount of starting categories. You can put the numbers you roll into any category. You can also get a few +1 stat bonus depending on your origin of your character.
